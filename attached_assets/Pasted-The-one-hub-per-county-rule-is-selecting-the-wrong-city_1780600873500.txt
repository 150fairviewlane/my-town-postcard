The one-hub-per-county rule is selecting the wrong city
from each county. It's picking the closest city in the
county rather than the most commercially significant one.

For Cleveland GA (30528):
  Hall County contains both Clermont AND Gainesville.
  Clermont (~1,500 people) is closer to Cleveland.
  Gainesville (~45,000 people) is the county seat and
  commercial hub.
  The system is selecting Clermont — wrong.
  It should select Gainesville.

  Habersham County contains Demorest, Clarkesville, Cornelia.
  Demorest (~1,800 people) is closest to Cleveland.
  Cornelia (~4,500) and Clarkesville are larger commercial hubs.
  The system is selecting Demorest — wrong.

════════════════════════════════════════════════
THE FIX
════════════════════════════════════════════════

In selectBestHubs(), change the within-county selection:

CURRENT (wrong):
  Sort all candidates by distance, pick first one per county

NEW (correct):
  1. Group all qualifying candidates by county
  2. For each county, select the candidate with the HIGHEST
     business count (not the closest)
  3. Then sort the selected per-county winners by distance
     from the dealer to determine county order
  4. Take the 4 closest counties (by their winning hub's
     distance from dealer)

In plain terms:
  - WHICH county gets selected = closest county
  - WHICH city represents that county = most businesses

This ensures Gainesville represents Hall County (most
businesses) even though Clermont is closer to Cleveland.
And Cornelia or Clarkesville represents Habersham County
rather than Demorest.

════════════════════════════════════════════════
ALSO FIX — MINIMUM CITY SIZE
════════════════════════════════════════════════

Even with the above fix, add a hard minimum:
If the best city in a county has fewer than 5,000 people
OR fewer than 50 postcard-relevant businesses within 5 miles:
  Skip that county entirely — do not force a hub from it
  Let the next closest county fill the slot instead

This prevents forcing Demorest or Clermont as a hub even
if they happen to be the only city in their county that
passes other filters.
