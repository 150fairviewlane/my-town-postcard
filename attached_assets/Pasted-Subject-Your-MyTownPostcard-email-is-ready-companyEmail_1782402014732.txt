### If you use Gmail

You can set this up in about 5 minutes, and it'll send through your own Gmail account — no new passwords to manage from us.

1. Go to Settings (gear icon) → See all settings → Accounts and Import
2. Under "Send mail as," click Add another email address
3. Enter your name and {{companyEmail}}, then click Next Step
4. For the SMTP server, enter:
   - SMTP Server: smtp.gmail.com
   - Username: your own Gmail address (not {{companyEmail}})
   - Password: you'll need a Google App Password — if you don't have one, go to myaccount.google.com/apppasswords, generate one, and paste it here instead of your regular password
   - Port: 587, with "Secured connection using TLS" selected
5. Click Add Account — a confirmation email will land in your inbox (it forwards there automatically). Open it and click the confirmation link.
6. Done! When composing a new email, click the From field to choose {{companyEmail}} instead of your personal address.
