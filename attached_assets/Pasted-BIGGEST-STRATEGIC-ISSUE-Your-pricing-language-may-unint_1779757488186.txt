“6-Issue Commitment”
“12-Issue Commitment”
That psychologically feels:
contractual
restrictive
risky

6-Issue Commitment
12-Issue Commitment

Current	Better

6-Issue Commitment	Growth Plan
12-Issue Commitment	Premium Visibility Plan

“Runs for 6 consecutive issues”
“Runs for 12 consecutive issues”
This feels MUCH less intimidating.

🔥 MOST IMPORTANT TECHNICAL HOLE
Your “issues_remaining” logic is dangerous.
Right now:
decrement on successful payment
But issues and payments are NOT the same thing.
Example:
payment succeeds
issue delayed
customer skips issue
production changes
Now your accounting drifts.
✅ Better approach
Instead of:
issues_remaining
Use:
commitment_total_issues
issues_fulfilled
Then:
remaining = total - fulfilled
And ONLY increment fulfilled when:
issue is actually finalized/mailed
NOT when payment succeeds.
This is VERY important operationally.

subscription

proof approval
actual print inclusion

subscription_issue_assignments
Fields:
subscription_id
issue_id

proof_status

skipped_reason

auditing becomes impossible

reprints become messy
This is a VERY important omission.
