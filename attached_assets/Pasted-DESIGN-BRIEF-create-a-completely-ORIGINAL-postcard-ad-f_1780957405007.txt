VISUAL CONSTRUCTION — MANDATORY:
(a) NO hard rectangular photo borders — use blob mask, brush-stroke cutout, diagonal slash, arch shape, or gradient vignette
(b) Background must have depth — atmospheric gradient, layered texture, or painterly wash. NEVER a flat solid color anywhere.
(c) THREE DEPTH PLANES — each must be visually distinct from the others:
PLANE 1 (background): Rich textured or atmospheric environment. Photographic depth or painterly quality. Tonal variation across the entire canvas. Never flat, never uniform.
PLANE 2 (mid-layer): Graphic design elements — color bands, overlapping shapes, brushwork, translucent overlays — that bridge background and foreground. This layer is what makes the ad feel professionally designed rather than assembled.
PLANE 3 (foreground): All text and logo elements at maximum contrast and sharpness. Drop shadows or dark-field backlighting mandatory on every text element.
(d) HERO PHOTO COMPOSITING — the photo must feel EMBEDDED in the composition, not placed on top like a sticker:
• Color grade the photo to match the overall palette
• Add rim lighting or edge glow that echoes the background color
• At least one photo edge must dissolve into the background via gradient fade, brushstroke mask, or organic vignette
• Cinematic depth of field — sharp subject, atmospheric background blur
(e) Every text element must have drop shadows, glows, or dark-field backlighting — no text floats on bare color
(f) FILL EVERY SQUARE INCH — no empty zones, no bare padding, no wasted canvas area
(g) Canvas boundary IS the ad boundary — no floating card illusion, no outer border or shadow frame
