PART 2 — AUTO-ASSIGN METRO ZIP CODES BY CITY NAME
════════════════════════════════════════════════

After updating the territory records above, auto-assign ZIP codes to
the metro territories using the ZIP's primary city name rather than
county boundary. Use this mapping:

For each ZIP in territory_zip_assignments that is currently unassigned
(or for ZIPs in the ga-zips GeoJSON that have no assignment yet),
look at the ZIP's NAME or city field in the GeoJSON properties and
assign it using these rules:

ASSIGN to GA-001 (Alpharetta/Milton/Roswell) if city name contains:
  Alpharetta, Milton, Roswell, Johns Creek, Mountain Park

ASSIGN to GA-002 (Sandy Springs/Buckhead/Dunwoody) if city contains:
  Sandy Springs, Buckhead, Dunwoody, Brookhaven, Chamblee, Doraville,
  Peachtree Corners (partial overlap — use GA-004 if Gwinnett FIPS)

ASSIGN to GA-003 (Atlanta/Midtown) if city contains:
  Atlanta (and ZIP is in Fulton or DeKalb FIPS 13121 or 13089)

ASSIGN to GA-004 (Duluth/Peachtree Corners/Norcross) if city contains:
  Duluth, Norcross, Peachtree Corners, Berkeley Lake

ASSIGN to GA-005 (Lawrenceville/Snellville/Lilburn) if city contains:
  Lawrenceville, Snellville, Lilburn, Stone Mountain, Tucker,
  Grayson, Loganville (Gwinnett side)

ASSIGN to GA-006 (Buford/Sugar Hill/Braselton) if city contains:
  Buford, Sugar Hill, Dacula, Braselton, Hoschton, Pendergrass,
  Flowery Branch (Gwinnett side only — Hall County Flowery Branch
  stays in GA-026 Hall County)

ASSIGN to GA-007 (Marietta/Kennesaw/Acworth) if city contains:
  Marietta, Kennesaw, Acworth, Canton (Cobb side only)

ASSIGN to GA-008 (Smyrna/Mableton/Powder Springs) if city contains:
  Smyrna, Mableton, Powder Springs, Austell, Vinings, Clarkdale,
  Lithia Springs (Cobb side)

ASSIGN to GA-009 (Decatur/Tucker/Clarkston) if city contains:
  Decatur, Tucker, Clarkston, Avondale Estates, Pine Lake,
  Scottdale, Panthersville

ASSIGN to GA-010 (Stonecrest/Lithonia) if city contains:
  Stonecrest, Lithonia, Redan, Ellenwood (DeKalb side)

ASSIGN to GA-096 (South Fulton) if city contains:
  Union City, Fairburn, Palmetto, Chattahoochee Hills, Tyrone
  (Fulton side only — Tyrone in Fayette stays in GA-018)

For any ZIP that does not match a city name rule above, fall back to
the county-based assignment from the existing seed logic. Only skip
a ZIP if it genuinely cannot be determined.

After running the city-name assignment, log how many ZIPs were assigned
to each metro territory and how many remain unassigned. The goal is
fewer than 10 unassigned ZIPs across all metro territories.
