OVERVIEW — WHY THIS IS DIFFERENT FROM V4
════════════════════════════════════════════════

In v4, AI generated the entire ad as one image. This caused:

- Text spelling errors
- Menu items cut off by overlapping elements
- Slow generation (60+ seconds)
- Layout inconsistency between generations

In v5, the architecture is split:

- AI generates ONLY the background atmosphere layer (textures, lighting, decorative elements, NO text)
- All text (business name, tagline, menu, coupon, phone, address, QR) is rendered as crisp HTML/CSS
- The AI background fades in behind the HTML text layer
- Result: ~20 seconds, perfect text accuracy, consistent layout every time
