The AI territory prompt needs one targeted fix. The AI is
selecting distant neighboring counties (Lumpkin/Union)
instead of the nearest neighboring county (Habersham) when
expanding beyond the home county.

Cleveland GA result is:
  Cleveland + Helen (White County) ✓
  Dahlonega (Lumpkin County) ✗ — wrong neighbor
  Blairsville (Union County) ✗ — wrong neighbor, 1 hour away

Expected result:
  Cleveland + Helen (White County) ✓
  Cornelia + Clarkesville (Habersham County) ✓ — nearest neighbor

THE FIX — UPDATE THE PROMPT
════════════════════════════════════════════════

In getAITerritoryHubs(), find the SELECTION RULES section
of the prompt and replace rule #2 with this more explicit
version:

OLD rule 2:
"Only expand to a neighboring county when the home county
has fewer than 4 qualifying cities. Fill that neighbor
county completely before moving to a third."

NEW rule 2:
"Only expand to a neighboring county when the home county
has fewer than 4 qualifying cities. When expanding, choose
the GEOGRAPHICALLY NEAREST neighboring county to the dealer's
home city — not the county with the most well-known city.
Measure by driving distance or straight-line distance between
county seats. Fill that nearest neighbor county completely
(add all its qualifying cities) before considering a third
county. Only move to a third county if the nearest two
counties together still have fewer than 4 qualifying cities."

Also add this as a new rule #8:
"PROXIMITY IS THE PRIMARY CRITERION for neighbor county
selection. Example: if a dealer is in Cleveland GA (White
County), the neighboring counties in order of proximity are:
Habersham (east, ~15 miles), Lumpkin (west, ~20 miles),
Hall (south, ~20 miles), Towns (north, ~25 miles), Union
(northwest, ~30 miles). Habersham County must be the first
expansion county — not Lumpkin or Union which are farther."

════════════════════════════════════════════════
ALSO ADD — EXPLICIT COUNTY INSTRUCTION
════════════════════════════════════════════════

At the end of the prompt, just before the JSON schema,
add this geographic anchor:

"For ${city}, ${state}: The home county is [resolve from
ZIP or city name]. List the neighboring counties in order
of proximity to ${city}, then fill from the closest one."

This forces the AI to explicitly reason about proximity
before selecting neighbor counties.
